Starting Small

In 2012, we landed as Lean Staffing Solutions in Cartagena, Colombia, with the mission to create the best and most seamless nearshore experience for U.S.-based companies looking to set up their satellite offices. Over time, we expanded our expertise, diversified industries, added new business solutions, and in 2020, rebranded as Lean Solutions Group. This name change reflects our growth, adaptability, and comprehensive client solutions.

By working with us, operational costs would immediately reduce, and being in the same time zone, we would provide an uninterrupted workflow, which was essential to our clients' success.
