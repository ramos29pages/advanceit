White Paper: Technology Deployment for Stonex's New Offices in Bogotá, Colombia

Client: Stonex (U.S. Financial Institution)

Location: Bogotá, Colombia - Completion Date: March, 2025

Introduction:

Advance Technology Projects is proud to have played a key role in supporting Stonex, a leading U.S. financial institution, in the successful launch of its new corporate offices in Bogotá, Colombia. This project involved the procurement, configuration, and deployment of cutting-edge technology solutions, ensuring that Stonex’s employees could operate efficiently and securely in their new environment. Our end-to-end service covered everything from hardware provisioning to on-site setup, ensuring optimal performance and user experience.

Project Scope and Deliverables:

The deployment covered the full range of IT infrastructure required for a modern financial institution, with a focus on high-performance computing, collaboration tools, and seamless connectivity.

Provisioned Equipment:
  • 150 Dell Latitude S7 (Intel Ci7, 32GB, 512GB SSD) – Secure and powerful devices for employee workstations.
  • 50 Dell Workstations (32GB, 1TB SSD) – High-performance desktops for advanced financial analysis and operations.
  • 200 Jabra Evolve2 Headsets – Noise-canceling headsets for optimal communication in financial transactions.
  • 400 Dell 24” Monitors – Dual-monitor setups for increased productivity across the office.
  • 200 Dell Docking Stations – Enabling flexible and efficient workstation setups.
  • 200 Dell Wireless Keyboard & Mouse Combos – Enhancing workspace ergonomics and efficiency.
  • Logitech TapScheduler Meeting Rooms – A smart scheduling solution compatible with Microsoft Teams and Zoom for streamlined meeting management.
  • Rally System for Large Conference Rooms (PTZ Pro Cameras) – High-quality video conferencing solutions for international collaboration.

Implementation and Deployment:

  1. Planning & Coordination:
    • Developed a comprehensive work plan detailing timelines, support services, and response protocols.
    • Coordinated with Stonex’s IT team to align configurations with global security and compliance standards.
  2. Hardware Procurement & Configuration:
    • Sourced and pre-configured all IT equipment to meet Stonex’s security and performance requirements.
    • Ensured all devices were tested for seamless integration with Stonex’s financial systems.
  3. On-Site Installation & Setup:
    • Deployed a dedicated team to oversee installation, setup, and network configuration.
    • Implemented dual-monitor workstations and ensured connectivity to docking stations.
    • Configured and optimized meeting room technology for high-quality virtual collaboration.
  4. Testing & Quality Assurance:
    • Conducted rigorous performance testing on all installed devices.
    • Ensured compatibility with Stonex’s secure financial platforms and data protection policies.
    • Delivered ongoing IT support for troubleshooting and optimization.

Key Benefits for Stonex:

  • ✔ High-Performance Computing: Ensuring that financial analysts and traders can operate efficiently with powerful workstations.
  • ✔ Seamless Connectivity: Secure and optimized IT infrastructure supporting remote access and collaboration.
  • ✔ Enhanced Collaboration Tools: Cutting-edge meeting room technology enabling high-quality virtual interactions.
  • ✔ Ergonomic & Efficient Workstations: Thoughtfully designed setups to enhance employee productivity.
  • ✔ End-to-End IT Support: From procurement to post-installation, ensuring a seamless transition.
  • ✔ Security & Compliance: Full adherence to Stonex’s global cybersecurity and data protection standards.

Conclusion:

Advance Technology Projects successfully delivered a comprehensive IT infrastructure solution for Stonex’s Bogotá office, enabling seamless operations and a high-performance working environment. By leveraging the latest technology and our expertise in IT deployment, we provided a turnkey solution that empowered Stonex to focus on their core financial operations while ensuring their employees had the best tools to succeed.
